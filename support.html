Frequently Asked Questions

Are your products safe for sensitive skin? +
Yes, all our products are dermatologist-tested and formulated without synthetic fragrances or harsh sulfates. We recommend our Bakuchiol range specifically for sensitive skin.
Where are your products manufactured? +
All Aura Botanics products are formulated and manufactured in small batches in our Jaipur, Rajasthan facility to ensure freshness and potency.
Are your products vegan and cruelty-free? +
Absolutely. We are PETA-certified cruelty-free and 100% of our formulas are vegan. We do not use honey, beeswax, or any animal-derived ingredients.
How should I store my serums? +
To maintain maximum potency of the Vitamin C and botanical oils, we recommend storing them in a cool, dark place away from direct sunlight. Refrigeration is optional but can feel great during application!
